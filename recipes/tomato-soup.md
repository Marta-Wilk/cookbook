# Tomato Soup

**Serves:** 4
**Time:** 30 min
**Tags:** soup, tomatoes, quick

## Ingredients

- 800g ripe tomatoes, roughly chopped (or 2 cans chopped tomatoes)
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, sliced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 500ml vegetable stock
- 1 tsp sugar
- handful fresh basil
- salt and pepper to taste
- cream to serve (optional)

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Sauté onion for 5 minutes until soft.
2. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
3. Add tomatoes, stock, and sugar. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 15 minutes.
4. Blend until smooth with a stick blender.
5. Season with salt, pepper, and basil. Serve with cream if desired.

## Notes

Best made with ripe summer tomatoes. Pairs well with crusty bread.
