# Chicken Chop with Mashed Potatoes and Salad

**Serves:** 2
**Time:** 45 min
**Tags:** lunch, chicken, potatoes, salad

## Ingredients

- 2 chicken breast fillets (about 200g each)
- 600g potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 50ml milk, warmed
- 30g butter
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 romaine lettuce heart, chopped
- 1 medium tomato, diced
- half a cucumber, sliced
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place chicken fillets between two sheets of cling film and pound to an even 1.5 cm thickness.
2. Rub chicken with olive oil, garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper. Set aside to marinate for 10 minutes.
3. Boil potatoes in salted water for 15–20 minutes until tender. Drain well.
4. Mash potatoes with butter and warm milk until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
5. While potatoes cook, heat a grill pan or heavy frying pan over medium-high heat.
6. Cook chicken for 4–5 minutes per side until golden and cooked through (no pink inside).
7. Toss lettuce, tomato, and cucumber with lemon juice, a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper.
8. Serve chicken alongside mashed potatoes and salad.

## Notes

Pounding the chicken to even thickness ensures it cooks evenly without drying out.
Rest the cooked chicken for 2 minutes before serving.
