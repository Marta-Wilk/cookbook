# Spaghetti Bolognese

**Serves:** 4
**Time:** 45 min
**Tags:** pasta, italian, beef, classic

## Ingredients

- 400g spaghetti
- 500g minced beef
- 2 cans (400g each) chopped tomatoes
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- salt and pepper to taste
- parmesan to serve

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to the boil.
2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
4. Add minced beef, increase heat to high, and brown for 5–7 minutes, breaking it up.
5. Stir in tomatoes and oregano. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 20 minutes.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Cook spaghetti according to packet instructions. Drain, reserving a cup of pasta water.
8. Toss spaghetti with sauce, adding a splash of pasta water if needed. Serve with parmesan.

## Notes

Leftovers keep for 3 days in the fridge and freeze well for up to 3 months.
