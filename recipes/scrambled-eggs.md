# Scrambled Eggs

**Serves:** 2
**Time:** 10 min
**Tags:** breakfast, eggs, quick

## Ingredients

- 4 large eggs
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp milk
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Crack eggs into a bowl, add milk, salt and pepper, and whisk until combined.
2. Melt butter in a non-stick pan over low-medium heat.
3. Pour in the egg mixture and let it sit for 30 seconds until the edges start to set.
4. Using a spatula, gently fold the eggs from the edges toward the centre, creating large soft curds.
5. Remove from heat while still slightly underdone — residual heat will finish them.
6. Serve immediately on toast.

## Notes

The key to creamy scrambled eggs is low heat and patience. Do not stir constantly — fold slowly.
