# Oatmeal with Berries

**Serves:** 2
**Time:** 10 min
**Tags:** breakfast, oats, vegetarian, healthy

## Ingredients

- 100g rolled oats
- 400ml milk
- 100g mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
- 1 tbsp honey
- pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Combine oats, milk, and salt in a small saucepan over medium heat.
2. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5–7 minutes until the oats are soft and the mixture is creamy.
3. Divide into bowls and top with berries.
4. Drizzle honey over the top and serve warm.

## Notes

Frozen berries work just as well as fresh. Stir them in during the last minute of cooking to warm them through.
